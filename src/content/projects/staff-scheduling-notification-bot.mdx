problem: "A professional services business with 25 part-time staff was managing weekly rosters in a shared Excel file. An admin staff member spent 2 hours every day sending WhatsApp reminders for upcoming shifts, following up on leave requests, and manually updating the schedule when swaps occurred. Errors were common and staff occasionally missed shifts due to miscommunication."
solution: "Built a scheduling system using Notion as the roster database and n8n as the automation engine. Rosters are entered in Notion once per week. n8n polls the schedule daily and sends personalised Telegram messages to each staff member 24 hours and 2 hours before their shift. A Telegram bot handles leave request submissions — staff send a command, the bot logs the request in Notion, and managers receive an instant notification with one-click approval."
